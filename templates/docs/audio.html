Audio: Module for managing the queue.

Bass: Toggle the bass effect.

Usage: -bass
Output: Toggles the bass audio effect.

Default: Restore audio effects to default.

Usage: -default
Output: Resets all effects to default.

Earrape: Toggle the earrape effect.

Usage: -earrape
Output: Enables the earrape audio effect.

Echo: Toggle the echo effect.

Usage: -echo
Output: Enables the echo audio effect.

Effects: Show audio effects.

Usage: -effects
Output: Shows all available effects

Muffle: Toggle the muffle effect.

Usage: -muffle
Output: Toggles the muffle audio effect.

Nightcore: Toggle the nightcore effect.

Usage: -nightcore
Output: Enables the nightcore audio effect.

Phaser: Toggle the phaser effect.

Usage: -phaser
Output: Toggles the phaser audio effect.

Pitch: Alter the pitch of the player.

Usage: -pitch [pitch]
Output: Alter the pitch of the player.
Notes: The pitch must be between 0.5 and 2.0. Will output the current pitch if no new pitch is explicitly specified.

Robot: Toggle the robot effect.

Usage: -robot
Output: Toggles the robot audio effect.

Speed: Alter the speed of the player.

Usage: -speed [speed]
Alias: -tempo
Output: Speed up or slow down the player.
Notes: The speed must be between 0.5 and 2.0. Will output the current speed if no new speed is explicitly specified.

Treble: Toggle the treble effect.

Usage: -treble
Output: Toggles the treble audio effect.

Tremolo: Toggle the tremolo effect.

Usage: -tremolo
Output: Enables the tremolo audio effect.

Vibrato: Toggle the vibrato effect.

Usage: -vibrato
Output: Enables the vibrato audio effect.

Volume: Alter the volume.

Usage: -volume [volume]
Alias: -vol, -sound
Output: Changes the volume of the player.
Notes: Will show current volume if no new volume is specified.

Whisper: Toggle the whisper effect.

Usage: -whisper
Output: Toggles the whisper audio effect.

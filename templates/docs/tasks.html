Tasks: Module for handling all timed tasks.

Reminder: Set a reminder for yourself.

Usage: -remind <when>
Aliases: -reminder, -timer
Output: Reminds you after a specified amount of time. The input can be any direct date (e.g. YYYY-MM-DD) or a human readable offset.
Examples: - "next thursday at 3pm do something funny" - "do the dishes tomorrow" - "in 3 days do the thing" - "2d unmute someone" Times are in UTC.

Reminder Clear: Clear all your current reminders.

Usage: -remind clear
Output: Clears all reminders you have set.

Reminder Delete: Delete a reminder.

Usage: -remind delete [reminder ID]
Aliases: -remind remove, -remind cancel
Output: Deletes a reminder by its ID.
Notes: To get a reminder ID, use `-reminder list`

Reminder List: Show all your current reminders.

Usage: -remind list
Alias: -remind show
Output: Shows all your pendind reminders
